Recent developments in Artificial Intelligence (AIED) have created high expectations for the future impact of AI on education and learning. These expectations were often based on a misunderstanding of current technical possibilities, ignorance of the latest artificial intelligence in education and an extremely narrow vision of the tasks of education in society. In this article, we provide an overview of existing educational AI systems and their pedagogical and educational prerequisites. We develop a typology of AIED systems and describe the different ways in which AIED can be used in education and learning, show how these are based on different interpretations of what AI and education are or can be, and discuss potential obstacles to the AIED highway ..
